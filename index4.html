Libation Loader



Welcome to Libation Loader, a website built for the thirsty. You're thirsty aren't you? Me too my friend, you've come to the right place. Libation Loader allows you to search over 3,000 cocktails inclusive of virgin cocktail offerings for both the weak and the weak from last night. Each cocktail includes the ingredient list and a recipe to concoct the creation yourself.
We currently offer four methods by which to search: by cocktail name, by a specific ingredient, by drink category and completely random for those of you feeling adventurous. The results are currently limited to 250 cocktails so we've added both an alcoholic/non-alcoholic filter (very helpful for entertainers looking to make something fun for guests) as well as a liquor filter with which you can filter your search results to only those recipes which contain a specific liquor.
You may notice many cocktails do not yet have pictures or that there is a cocktail you would like to see on the list. Well hot damn! We would like to see it on the list too! Please send any picture or recipe additions to the Drink Curator. Thanks for stopping by and please enjoy responsibly. Cheers!
